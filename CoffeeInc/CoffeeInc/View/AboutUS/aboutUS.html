HOW WE STARTED

It all started in 2017. At the time, Coffee Inc. was just a group of friends who loved coffee and visited several coffee shops in New York, getting to know their different flavors and aromas. However, during 2018, they started buying some coffees and roasting them at home, experimenting with different blends.

During 2019, two of the friends suffered a financial setback and the third decided to join them and invest in a coffee shop. Putting into practice everything they had known in the last three years, the coffee shop began to sell coffees of extreme quality and flavor, always seeking to please coffee lovers, as well as their creators.
